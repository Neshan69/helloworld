About Aroma Café

Aroma Café celebrates the vibrant flavors and culinary heritage of Nepal, bringing together traditional recipes crafted with love and the freshest locally-sourced ingredients. Since our first roast, we’ve been dedicated to blending cozy hospitality with innovative tastes—serving up everything from beloved classics like momo and dal bhat to contemporary delights for the adventurous foodie.

  • 🌱 Locally-sourced, fresh produce and spices
  • ☕ Handcrafted coffees & teas with signature blends
  • 🧑‍🍳 Warm, inviting space for friends, family, and food lovers
  • 🏆 Proud supporter of local farmers and culinary artisans
